Backend env (optional features):
- OPENWEATHER_API_KEY — current weather proxy /v1/weather/current
- DEEPSEEK_API_KEY — AI chat /v1/ai/chat (optional DEEPSEEK_API_URL, DEEPSEEK_MODEL)
- FIREBASE_PROJECT_ID — FCM HTTP v1 (push social)
- FIREBASE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON — JSON complet service account (un singur rând / string), scope firebase.messaging
