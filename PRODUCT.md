# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Zvelt is for gym users who want a fast, reliable training log, a solid nutrition diary, and light social motivation. The core user is someone who opens the app at the gym, between sets, at meals, or after a run, and needs the interface to be quick, readable, and trustworthy.

## Product Purpose

Zvelt helps users track strength workouts, nutrition, cardio, streaks, progress, and friend challenges without turning the experience into a game or a corporate dashboard. Success means the user can start a workout quickly, log sets confidently, add food without friction, see real progress, and share or join challenges when they choose.

## Brand Personality

Premium, focused, supportive. Zvelt should feel like a serious fitness companion with a clean product surface, clear data, and a friendly coach mascot used sparingly for motivation and empty states.

## Anti-references

Do not make it look like a generic crypto dashboard, a playful mobile game, a noisy social app, or a template fitness landing page. Avoid random gradients, excessive glow, decorative blur, fake metrics, cluttered cards, and motion that slows logging.

## Design Principles

1. Preserve trust: every number shown must come from real data or an honest empty/loading state.
2. Optimize the gym moment: workout logging must stay fast, touch-friendly, and readable under pressure.
3. Use one visual language: cards, buttons, inputs, icon tiles, and sheets should feel like one system.
4. Motivate without noise: mascot, streaks, and challenges should support progress, not distract from it.
5. Respect launch quality: accessibility, dynamic text, loading, error, and offline states matter as much as visuals.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, 44pt minimum touch targets, clear labels for icon-only controls, predictable back behavior, reduced-motion support where animations are added, and layouts that survive larger system text sizes.
